Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Flaggy Fun app (herein referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

The Application does not require a registration or login. Your game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device using Local Storage and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. Third Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help us improve the Application and our service. The Application uses third-party services that declare their own Privacy Policy.

Below are the links to the Privacy Policy of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob

3. Advertising

We display advertisements in the Application to help fund the service. These ads are served by Google AdMob. Google may use advertising identifiers from your device to serve relevant ads. We strive to configure our ad settings to be appropriate for a general audience, including children.

4. Children's Privacy

The Application is designed to be kid-friendly. We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. We do not collect personally identifiable information from any user. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

5. Cookies / Local Storage

The Application uses "cookies" or similar technologies (like Shared Preferences on Android or Local Storage on Web) to store your high scores and music preferences locally. These are not used to track you across other websites or apps.

6. Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. Since we do not collect personal data, the risk of data breach is minimized.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email.
